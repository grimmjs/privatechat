Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-02

1. What we collect

We store the minimum information needed to operate the service:

  • Username and a randomly generated user code.
  • Hashed password (scrypt with per-user salt).
  • Hashed account-recovery code (single-use).
  • Optional avatar, bio and status text that you provide.
  • Friend relationships and active session metadata (device label, IP, last-seen).
  • Encrypted message ciphertext and IV. The server cannot decrypt these.
  • Security audit log (login events, password changes, 2FA changes, session revokes).

2. End-to-end encryption

Messages and file attachments are encrypted in your browser with AES-GCM 256 using a key derived per conversation. The server only relays opaque ciphertext and never has access to your messages, files, or derivation key.

3. What we do NOT collect

  • No tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or cross-site cookies.
  • No analytics SDKs.
  • No phone number, email or real-name requirement.

4. Data retention

Messages are stored encrypted until you delete them. Disappearing messages are purged from the server within sixty seconds of expiry. The audit log is retained for security review and is cleared when you delete your account.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

You can export all data we hold about your account from Settings → Data & privacy → Export. You can permanently delete your account and all related records from the same screen — this action is irreversible.

6. Age

The service is not intended for users under 13. By creating an account you confirm you are at least 13.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or GDPR requests, please open an issue in the project repository.
